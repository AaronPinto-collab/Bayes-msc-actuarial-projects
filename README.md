This repository contains selected academic projects completed during my MSc in Actuarial Science at Bayes Business School.

The projects cover several areas of actuarial and quantitative finance, including:

- Financial Mathematics (CM1)
- Insurance Risk Modelling
- Time Series Analysis
- Survival Analysis
- Multivariate Statistical Methods
- Predictive Analytics
- Financial Market Modelling
- Agent-Based Risk Simulation

Most projects were completed as group coursework, while the CM1 Financial Mathematics projects were completed individually.
